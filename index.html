OutfitBroo Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Information We Collect

OutfitBroo collects the following information: - Email address for account creation - Photos of clothing items you upload - Usage data to improve our service

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: - Provide AI powered outfit suggestions - Save your wardrobe items - Improve our styling recommendations

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase. Photos are stored in Firebase Storage and linked to your account only.

Third Party Services

We use the following third party services: - Google Firebase for authentication and storage - Google Gemini AI for outfit analysis - These services have their own privacy policies

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data. Each user can only access their own wardrobe photos and data.

User Rights

You have the right to: - Delete your account and all associated data - Request your data at any time - Contact us at outfitbroo@gmail.com

Children Privacy

OutfitBroo is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy contact us at: outfitbroo@gmail.com
